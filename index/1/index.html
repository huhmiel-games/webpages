K438-B NEWS

January 24, 2020 by BlunT76

K438-B is a metroidvania really similar to metroid. Development started a while back, and is far from finished.

Actually there's already 4 explorable zone: forest, lava, water and snow.

All the player abilities are almost done but needs some fixes here and there. Some enemies are ready, others not yet implemented.

About bosses, only the forest mini-boss is done. Lava and water bosses are almost done but already ingame.
